→ Weigh each morning, same conditions (post-toilet, pre-food)
→ Fat stays above 50g/day — hormonal health depends on it
→ Pre-workout: small carb snack 45–60 mins before HIIT or runs
→ Post-workout: protein within 30 mins after training
→ If losing >1.5kg in week 1, add 100 kcal back on training days
→ Energy dips and hunger are expected — this is an aggressive plan
